Help for wherever you are on your Anti-Fraud credit card system journey

  • The first section predictornecessary details that you need to be aware of are:
  • Average Amount/transaction/day : Enter the average amount of money spent per transaction on a daily basis. Please provide the average value for all transactions made in a single day.
  • Transaction_amount : Enter the amount of money involved in each individual transaction. Please input the specific amount spent or received for each transaction.
  • Is declined : Check this box if the transaction was declined. If the transaction was successful, leave this box unchecked.
  • Total Number of declines/day : Enter the total count of transactions that were declined in a single day. Provide the number of transactions that were not processed successfully.
  • isForeignTransaction : Enter either 0 or 1 in this these field if you are making transaction to person in other country then enter 1 or else within same country enter 0
  • isHighRiskCountry : Enter either 0 or 1 in this these field if the country to which your are making transaction has hihg risk of fradulent transaction then enter 1 or else enter 0
    • Daily_chargeback_avg_amt :> Enter the average amount of money involved in chargebacks per day. Chargebacks occur when customers dispute transactions. Please provide the average amount for all chargebacks in a single day.
  • 6_month_avg_chbk_amt : Enter the average amount of money involved in chargebacks over the past 6 months. This represents the average financial impact of all chargebacks during this period.
  • 6-month_chbk_freq : Enter the frequency of chargebacks over the past 6 months. This represents how often chargebacks occurred during this period. Provide the total count of chargebacks received during the last 6 months.
